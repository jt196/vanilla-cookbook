    Grilled Asparagus

    Prep Time
    2 hours
    Cook Time
    7 minutes
    Total Time
    2 h 7 m
    Serves
    4  People

    Ingredients

    Recipe makes 4 Servings

    2 Tbsp olive oil
    2 Tbsp H-E-B Balsamic Vinegar of Modena
    1 Lb asparagus, trimmed
    2 Tbsp Adams Reserve House All-Purpose Rub
    Check All
    Uncheck All
    Add to list

    Instructions

    1
    Combine and toss oil, vinegar and asparagus in a plastic bag; chill 1-2 hours.
    2
    Remove asparagus from oil mixture, and season with rub.
    3
    Grill asparagus over medium heat 5-7 minutes until crisp and tender.
